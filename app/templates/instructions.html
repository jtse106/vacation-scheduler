How To Use It

Instructions

Quick guidance for physicians and administrators.

For physicians

Schedule vacation

Use the Schedule Vacation button in the sidebar or homepage hero. Pick your dates manually, or describe the request in plain language and let the assistant translate it. If every slot is already full, your request stays on the waitlist until space opens.

Understand blocked dates

Protected holidays appear directly on the calendar. Those dates remain blank and cannot be booked for VL.

Use delegation when needed

On Request History, you can authorize another physician to manage your schedule. Incoming delegations also appear there so you can see whose calendar you may edit.

Trade holiday assignments

On Request History, choose the year, select your holiday, select the other physician, and offer a same-category trade. The schedule updates after the invited physician accepts, and pending offers may be canceled by the sender.

For administrators

Create users and notify them

The Admin Console lets you create physicians and admins, set annual VL limits, remove accounts, reset passwords, and email account details automatically when SMTP is configured.

Control protected holidays

Use the Holidays panel to adjust major and minor holiday ranges when local rules differ from the generated defaults.

Review every change

The admin logs include a high-level activity log and a field-by-field change log so you can trace exactly what changed, by whom, and when.

Export the annual table

Use the Export tab to produce the physician-by-date VL matrix and download it as CSV.

Spring Break Guidelines

Spring Break is defined as the last week of March through the first 2 weeks of April. To allow for increased availability of requests during Spring Break weeks, physicians who have Spring Break off for the current year must wait until November 1 of the calendar year before requesting Spring Break for the following year.

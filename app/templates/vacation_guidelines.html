Policy

Vacation guidelines

Use these local rules together with the protected holiday schedule.

Guaranteed VL slots

Vacation is scheduled immediately when open slots exist. If every slot is full, the request is stored on the waitlist in entry order and is promoted automatically when space opens. Protected holiday dates remain blocked.

Holiday protections

Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Year's, Memorial Day, July 4th, and Labor Day are protected by default. Administrators can adjust the exact date ranges as needed.

Delegation

Each physician may update only their own schedule unless another physician explicitly grants delegation in the Request History page.

Holiday trades

Holiday assignments may be traded only after the receiving physician accepts the offer. Major holidays trade with major holidays, and minor holidays trade with minor holidays.

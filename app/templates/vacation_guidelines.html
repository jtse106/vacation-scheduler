Vacation Guidelines

Department vacation request rules and Spring Break policy.

Vacation Request Guidelines

Vacation requests may be submitted up to 1 year in advance and are processed on a first-come, first-served basis.

Up to 6 physicians may be scheduled off on any given day. Additional requests will be placed on a waitlist.

If a physician in one of the top 6 slots cancels their vacation, the remaining physicians will move up the waitlist accordingly.

Spring Break Guidelines

Spring Break is defined as the last week of March through the first 2 weeks of April.

Physicians who have Spring Break off during the current year must wait until November 1 to submit requests for Spring Break of the following year.

All other physicians may submit Spring Break vacation requests up to 1 year in advance, as usual.
